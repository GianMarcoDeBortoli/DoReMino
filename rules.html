Rules

Place the DòReMino tiles on the board to build a melody!

First of all, choose the mode you want to play in. (If you don't know what "mode" means, don't worry: it's like choosing which notes will be there, out of the ones that exist. Ionian is the one all of us are most familiar with, but the fun stands in trying them all and hearing the differences!)
Now it's time to play: open your ears.

Tiles will appear inside the bar, you can hear the sound of them by holding SHIFT and left-clicking over them. You can rotate a tile by DOUBLE CLICKING it. One tile may be placed on the board only if the first of the two colors matches the second one of the previous tile. Once you positioned a tile, you can also create a copy of it by holding ALT and left-clicking over it. This gives you the possibility to re-use tiles you particulary like.
You can listen to the melody so far by clicking the Play button.
If you don't like the ones available, you can change them by clicking Change Set button. Be careful because time will eventually run out!
Otherwise, you can end the game in advance with the Finish Game button.
Tip: COLORS are there to help you. If you like things to be more challenging, you can turn them off by selecting the difficulty to "expert".

One last thing: try to create a nice melody. Usually, a nice melody it's something you can easily sing and remember. You will receive some suggestions along the way!
